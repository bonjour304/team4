about this item:

Dried fruit is high in nutrients. One piece of dried fruit contains about the same amount of nutrients as fresh fruit, but concentrated in a small package. By weight, dried fruit contains up to 3.5 times more fiber, vitamins and minerals than fresh fruit. Therefore, one serving can provide a large percentage of the daily intake of many vitamins and minerals, such as folate.

Dried fruit usually contains a lot of fiber and is a good source of antioxidants especially polyphenols. Polyphenol antioxidants have been linked with health benefits such as improved blood flow, better digestive health, reduced oxidative damage, and reduced risk of many diseases. With some exceptions, vitamin C content can be significantly reduced when fruit is dried.
